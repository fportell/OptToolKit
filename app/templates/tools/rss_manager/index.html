About RSS Manager

RSS Manager helps you organize and manage RSS feed subscriptions for monitoring news, health alerts, and other content sources.

Features:

  • Full-text search across titles and URLs (FTS5-powered)
  • Advanced filtering by language, country, organization type, and scope
  • OPML export for importing into RSS readers
  • Bulk operations (delete multiple subscriptions at once)
  • Backup & restore with soft deletes
  • Multi-dimensional classification (language, country, subdivision, type, scope)
